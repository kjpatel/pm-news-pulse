## Summary

PM Skills 2.0 introduces new tools for product managers to red-team roadmaps and PRDs before launch, and to review AI-generated code for safety and intent alignment before shipping.

## Key Takeaways

- **Use /red-team-prd before reviews** to identify and test the riskiest assumptions in your strategy on your own time, rather than defending them to leadership a quarter later.
- **Run /ship-check on AI-built code** to document intent, audit implementation gaps, and create a reviewable shipping packet—turning 'the AI wrote it' into something a human can sign off on.
- **Install the free PM Skills marketplace** once in Claude Cowork or Code to automatically surface relevant frameworks and commands that match your workflow without manual catalog browsing.
- **Combine PM Skills with PM Brain** to move from generic frameworks to context-aware ones that leverage your actual product strategy, constraints, and positioning.
