# From Weeks to Hours: How Claude Design Compresses Product Discovery

**Source**: [Product Compass](https://www.productcompass.pm/p/claude-design-product-discovery)
**Author**: Pawel Huryn | **Date**: May 04, 2026

---

## Summary

Claude Design compresses product discovery from weeks to hours by enabling PMs to generate interactive prototypes directly from design systems and codebases, eliminating traditional design-to-engineering handoffs. This transforms the workflow from sequential (idea → designer mockup → engineer rebuild) to iterative (idea → Claude Design prototype → engineer continuation).

## Key Takeaways

- **Import your design system** into Claude Design by combining Figma libraries, Storybook, brand docs, or your codebase as a foundational source of truth that ensures all prototypes maintain brand consistency automatically.
- **Brief Claude Design with business context** including objective, persona, value proposition, job to be done, and analytics-backed common actions—this goes beyond prompt engineering and instruments strategic context that agents can leverage.
- **Iterate prototypes in hours not days** by switching to faster models (like Sonnet) for refinement work in chat, allowing PMs to make copy edits and layout adjustments without waiting for designer availability.
- **Hand off clickable prototypes to engineers** rather than static Figma files, enabling them to continue building from Claude Design output through Claude Code with no implementation drift or rebuilding required.
- **Run design system as a portable skill** by exporting it as a .zip file that can be shared across any agent (Claude Code, Cowork, Codex), scaling consistent design implementation across product lines and teams.
