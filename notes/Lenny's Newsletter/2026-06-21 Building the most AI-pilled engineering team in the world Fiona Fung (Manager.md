# Building the most AI-pilled engineering team in the world | Fiona Fung (Manager of the Claude Code and Cowork Teams)

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/building-the-most-ai-pilled-engineering)

## Summary

Fiona Fung, who leads Claude Code and Cowork at Anthropic, shares how her engineering team is shipping 8x more code than before by building AI-native workflows and discusses the fundamental shifts happening in software engineering roles and team management.

## Key Takeaways

- **Implement Claude routines** to transform how managers operate—use AI-assisted workflows to handle repetitive management tasks and free up time for higher-leverage activities like coaching and strategic decisions.
- **Measure productivity by code shipped per engineer**, not traditional metrics—Anthropic's teams now ship 8x more code per quarter than 2021-2025 baseline by leveraging AI coding tools effectively.
- **Redesign team workflows around AI capabilities** rather than forcing AI into existing processes—the biggest gains come from rethinking what engineers do day-to-day, not just adding AI tools on top.
- **Prioritize solving context-switching problems** as a manager—this is the critical unsolved challenge preventing teams from fully leveraging AI's capabilities for productivity gains.
- **Prepare for role transformation across engineering functions**—clearly understand which roles AI will transform next and help your team members evolve their skills accordingly rather than resist automation.
