# Netflix CPTO on AI and the future of product and tech roles | Elizabeth Stone

**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring Elizabeth Stone) | **Date**: Jul 19, 2026

## Summary

Elizabeth Stone, Netflix's Chief Product and Technology Officer, discusses how AI is fundamentally changing product and engineering roles, emphasizing that systems thinking, AI fluency, and managing AI-generated output quality are now critical leadership competencies.

## Key Takeaways

- **Develop systems thinking skills** - Recognize this as the most important capability to look for when hiring and developing teams, as it enables leaders to understand complex interdependencies in AI-augmented organizations.
- **Implement quality gates for AI output** - Create processes to manage the flood of AI-generated code and content without losing signal, treating AI fluency as a universal expectation rather than a specialized skill.
- **Reframe excellence as an operating system** - Move beyond individual performance metrics to building organizational systems that maintain quality standards and coherence as AI accelerates shipping velocity.
- **Make AI fluency universal, not role-specific** - Ensure all team members (engineers, designers, product managers) understand AI capabilities and limitations rather than treating it as an expert-only competency.
