## Summary

Claude Cowork is Anthropic's desktop tool that enables non-coders to automate their workday by connecting to apps like Gmail, Slack, and Notion, using a "brain" file strategy and shared project memory to build consistent AI assistants without writing code.

## Key Takeaways

- **Create a brain file** with your preferences, work style, and context that Claude reads every time to understand how you work and maintain consistency across all interactions.
- **Connect one-click integrations** to Gmail, Slack, Notion, and Google Calendar to give Claude permission to take real action (with safety guardrails) instead of just suggesting changes.
- **Build a sub-advisory-board skill** by spinning up three AI agents with different personas to review your work from multiple perspectives and improve decision quality.
- **Use scheduled tasks** to automate recurring workflows like morning debriefs that read your email, Slack, and calendar at set times, creating a daily operating system.
- **Leverage shared project memory** instead of individual chat threads to maintain context and consistency, enabling high-quality AI outputs that improve over time.
