## Summary

Browser and computer use in Codex (OpenAI's desktop app) enables AI agents to interact with your digital environment—from QA testing and form filling to shopping and managing devices—with examples showing how under-prompting often yields better results than detailed instructions.

## Key Takeaways

- **Combine desktop app with Chrome extension** for optimal browser and computer use workflows; the Codex desktop app plus Chrome extension is the most reliable combination for hands-free automation.
- **Under-prompt frontier models for better results** rather than providing exhaustive step-by-step instructions; let capable AI agents figure out the approach to achieve your goal.
- **Use persona-impersonation testing** to surface UX friction points you can't see as the builder; have someone else (like your husband) impersonate a user type to uncover hidden issues.
- **Automate high-friction, repetitive tasks** like inbox management, form filling, and QA testing that you would otherwise procrastinate on or never complete thoroughly.
