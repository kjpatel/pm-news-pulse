## Summary

Community Wisdom shares practical advice from Lenny's Slack community on screening AI-generated content in hiring, finding voice transcription alternatives, choosing between multi-agent pipelines and MCPs, and other timely AI implementation challenges.

## Key Takeaways

- **Screen for AI slop in hiring** by developing evaluation criteria that detect AI-generated responses and prioritize candidates who demonstrate original thinking and domain expertise.
- **Evaluate voice transcription tools beyond Whisper** by testing alternatives like AssemblyAI or Deepgram for your specific use case, as no single tool works optimally across all scenarios.
- **Choose multi-agent pipelines vs. MCP based on complexity** - use MCP for lightweight integrations and standardized protocols, but build custom multi-agent systems when you need sophisticated orchestration and state management.
- **Stay current with AI tooling** by regularly reassessing your tech stack as new solutions emerge, since the AI landscape evolves rapidly and your initial choices may be quickly outdated.
