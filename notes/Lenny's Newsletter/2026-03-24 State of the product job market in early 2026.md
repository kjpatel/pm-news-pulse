# State of the product job market in early 2026

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/state-of-the-product-job-market-in-372)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky | **Date**: Mar 24, 2026

## Summary

Despite negative headlines, PM job openings are at their highest point in over three years with AI roles experiencing explosive growth and tech headcount expanding across the industry. This episode provides an optimistic fourth biannual update on the tech job market, breaking down seven key trends reshaping the job landscape in early 2026.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor AI role growth**: AI positions are experiencing hockey-stick growth patterns, presenting a significant opportunity for PMs to upskill and position themselves in this rapidly expanding category.
- **Leverage timing for negotiations**: With PM job openings at their highest point in 3+ years, use this supply-demand advantage to negotiate better roles, compensation, and career growth opportunities.
- **Study market trends**: Understand current patterns across design and software engineering roles to anticipate skills gaps and adjacent opportunities that could enhance your marketability.
- **Adapt to remote opportunities**: Track surprising trends in remote job opportunities to expand your job search geography and access roles previously limited to specific locations.
