## Summary

This Community Wisdom column curates insights from Lenny's Slack community on practical topics including team-based vibe coding, interviewing techniques that surface genuine signal, and gaps in leadership development.

## Key Takeaways

- **Adopt vibe coding as a team practice** to improve code quality and team dynamics by making coding sessions collaborative rather than solitary.
- **Redesign your interview process** to focus on surfacing real signal about candidate capabilities rather than relying on traditional assessment methods.
- **Audit your leadership offsites** to identify and address missing elements that could improve strategic alignment and team cohesion among leaders.
