## Summary

Intercom doubled their engineering velocity in 9 months by going all-in on Claude Code across their entire organization, implementing telemetry infrastructure to measure adoption, and building a skills repository with automated quality standards. This transformation involved not just engineers, but designers, PMs, and TPMs shipping code together.

## Key Takeaways

- **Measure AI adoption systematically** by implementing telemetry infrastructure (like Honeycomb) to track skill usage, quality metrics, and per-engineer productivity across hundreds of developers.
- **Build reusable skills with guardrails** by creating a centralized repository with automated hooks that enforce engineering standards, enabling rapid AI-assisted development without sacrificing code quality.
- **Treat AI tooling costs as investments** rather than expenses—factor in reduced cycle times and doubled throughput when evaluating the ROI of AI coding tools for your engineering team.
- **Prepare your product for agents** by designing APIs as CLIs, creating helpful hints for agent navigation, and building ephemeral APIs that anticipate how agents will interact with your product.
- **Enable rapid adoption with clear accountability** by establishing permission frameworks that allow engineers to ship code confidently while maintaining quality standards through automated skill-based checks.
