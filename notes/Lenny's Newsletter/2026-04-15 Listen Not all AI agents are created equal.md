## Summary

This podcast episode breaks down the three architectural categories of AI agents and provides frameworks for selecting the right platform and measuring success for each type, addressing why traditional planning tools fail for AI agent initiatives.

## Key Takeaways

- **Understand agent architectures**: Learn the three distinct architectural categories of AI agents to make informed decisions about which type best fits your specific use case.
- **Abandon traditional planning**: Replace impact-effort matrices with agent-specific evaluation frameworks since conventional product planning tools break down for AI agent initiatives.
- **Match architecture to platform**: Select the appropriate platform based on your agent's architectural category rather than choosing platforms first and forcing architectures to fit.
- **Define tailored success metrics**: Establish ROI frameworks and success metrics specific to each architectural type instead of using one-size-fits-all product metrics.
- **Build course-correction loops**: Create mechanisms to quickly identify when you've chosen the wrong architecture and pivot before sunk costs become problematic.
