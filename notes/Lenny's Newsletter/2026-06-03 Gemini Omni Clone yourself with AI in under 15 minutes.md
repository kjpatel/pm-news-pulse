# Gemini Omni: Clone yourself with AI in under 15 minutes

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/gemini-omni-clone-yourself-with-ai)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky | **Date**: Jun 03, 2026

---

## Summary

Lenny demonstrates how to create a photorealistic AI avatar of yourself using Google Flow and Gemini Omni video generation, then uses it to produce a complete one-minute hype video in approximately 15 minutes with no video production experience required.

## Key Takeaways

- **Master the avatar creation workflow**: Scan your face with your phone, upload photos to Google Flow, and generate a consistent character model in under 5 minutes—no special equipment needed.
- **Use AI as your creative producer**: Leverage Flow to brainstorm storyboards and generate multiple video scenes with your AI clone, enabling non-video creators to produce professional-quality content.
- **Build in iterative refinement time**: Expect uncanny valley moments with emotion and physics accuracy; plan for troubleshooting (like accidentally generating images instead of videos) and use browser-based editors to stitch scenes together.
- **Unlock creative possibilities for non-technical creators**: Video AI tools eliminate traditional production barriers, allowing anyone to generate multi-scene videos with consistent characters and custom storyboards.
