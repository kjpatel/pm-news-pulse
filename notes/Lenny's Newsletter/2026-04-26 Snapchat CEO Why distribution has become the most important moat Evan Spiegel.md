# Snapchat CEO: Why distribution has become the most important moat | Evan Spiegel

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/snapchat-ceo-why-distribution-is)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring Evan Spiegel) | **Date**: Apr 26, 2026

---

## Summary

Snapchat CEO Evan Spiegel argues that distribution has become the most critical competitive moat for consumer technology companies, as pure software innovation is increasingly easy to replicate, and shares how Snap maintains competitive advantage through rapid iteration, design-led culture, and feature invention.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prioritize distribution as your moat.** Pure software products are no longer defensible; focus on building durable competitive advantages through distribution channels, user retention mechanisms, and ecosystem lock-in rather than feature parity alone.
- **Build small, empowered design teams without hierarchy.** Snap's 9-12 person design teams operate without titles and review hundreds of ideas weekly with leadership, enabling rapid innovation and faster shipping than larger, more bureaucratic organizations.
- **Expect rapid feature copying and build accordingly.** Since most features will be copied within months, design your business model and culture to innovate faster than competitors can replicate, making speed and iteration your true competitive advantage.
- **Use AI to accelerate designer productivity.** Designers at Snap are now shipping code directly using AI tools, compressing the design-to-implementation cycle and requiring fewer handoffs between disciplines.
- **Recognize human adoption as the real bottleneck.** AI technology capabilities will advance faster than society's comfort with using them; product strategy should focus on helping users become comfortable with AI rather than just building more AI features.
