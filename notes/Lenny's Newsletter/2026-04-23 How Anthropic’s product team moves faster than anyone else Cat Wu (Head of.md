# How Anthropic’s product team moves faster than anyone else | Cat Wu (Head of Product, Claude Code)

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/how-anthropics-product-team-moves)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring Cat Wu) | **Date**: Apr 23, 2026

---

## Summary

Cat Wu, Head of Product for Claude Code at Anthropic, reveals how the company accelerated its shipping cadence from months to days by maintaining mission alignment, building incomplete products ahead of model capabilities, and embracing a "just do things" ethos that eliminates organizational friction.

## Key Takeaways

- **Build incomplete products strategically** - Create features that don't fully work yet but will be ready when the next model version closes the capability gap, allowing you to ship faster than waiting for perfect alignment.
- **Use model introspection as a skill** - Ask AI models to reflect on and identify their own mistakes rather than relying solely on external evaluation, unlocking a powerful but underutilized capability for product improvement.
- **Eliminate friction through mission alignment** - Ensure your entire organization shares clear values and mission so decision-making happens faster without constant re-alignment conversations that slow down larger companies.
- **Adopt "just do things" as your operating principle** - In AI-native companies, action and iteration beat perfect planning; start shipping small experiments immediately rather than overanalyzing.
- **Invest in PM evals as a core skill** - Move beyond vibe checks to systematic evaluation frameworks that let you measure AI product improvements objectively and iterate with confidence.
