# Why a sabbatical can change everything

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/how-to-take-a-sabbatical)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring DJ DiDonna) | **Date**: Jul 21, 2026

---

## Summary

Taking a sabbatical—a significant break from work—can provide the mental and emotional space needed to reassess your life direction and unlock transformative personal growth. DJ DiDonna's research and book reveal that sabbaticals help people move off autopilot and reconnect with their authentic goals, whether that means staying in their current role with renewed purpose or pivoting to something entirely new.

## Key Takeaways

- **Identify your sabbatical archetype** (Achiever, Seeker, or Explorer) before taking time off to avoid traps like productivity obsession and instead align your break with what you genuinely need—whether that's accomplishment, self-discovery, or adventure.
- **Start small if a full sabbatical feels impossible**—even three months away from routine can create the mental shift needed to recognize when your work no longer fits who you want to become, as demonstrated by both Lenny and DJ's experiences.
- **Plan your sabbatical around your life stage and responsibilities** rather than age expectations; a gap year makes sense at any age, and the type of break you take should reflect your current circumstances, not societal norms.
- **Prepare to discover uncomfortable truths** about your work and life during your time off; sabbaticals often reveal that your current path no longer aligns with your values, which is valuable information worth acting on.
