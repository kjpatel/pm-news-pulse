## Summary

Anthropic's Head of Growth Amol Avasare reveals how Claude achieved unprecedented growth from $1B to $19B ARR in 14 months through strategic activation focus, AI-automated growth experiments, and a product-led growth strategy that inverts traditional PM-to-engineer ratios.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prioritize activation as your lever**: Focus on getting users to experience core value immediately, as activation is the single highest-leverage growth problem in AI products—more impactful than acquisition or retention.
- **Automate growth experiments with AI**: Build internal tools like Anthropic's CASH to use Claude for automating growth experiments, enabling rapid iteration and scaling of successful strategies without proportional team growth.
- **Flip your PM-to-engineer ratio**: As AI makes engineers exponentially more productive, consider hiring more PMs than engineers to maintain strategic direction and decision-making velocity.
- **Index toward big bets over incremental wins**: Adopt a 70/30 allocation favoring major initiatives over small optimizations, which compounds faster and drives breakthrough growth in AI products.
- **Use AI tools for team alignment detection**: Deploy Cowork and similar AI collaboration tools to monitor Slack conversations and identify team misalignment early, preventing execution friction.
