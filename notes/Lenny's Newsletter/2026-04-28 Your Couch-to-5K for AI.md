# Your Couch-to-5K for AI

## Summary

The article presents a progressive daily learning program called 'Couch-to-5K for AI' designed to help busy professionals build AI skills through small, manageable steps rather than overwhelming themselves with complex projects.

## Key Takeaways

- **Break tasks into smallest steps**: Instead of trying to master AI all at once, commit to one simple action per day—this overcomes procrastination by removing the barrier of 'getting started'
- **Use behavior-change psychology**: Apply proven fitness program principles (small wins → confidence → momentum) to AI skill-building rather than relying on willpower or finding large blocks of time
- **Start with 10-minute daily commitments**: The Couch-to-5K for AI program takes fewer than 10 minutes daily and is structured to progressively build Claude Code skills from beginner to practical application
- **Recognize 'busy' is often an excuse**: When people claim they're too busy to learn AI, the real barrier is usually something else—fear, unclear first step, or task overwhelm—which this program directly addresses
