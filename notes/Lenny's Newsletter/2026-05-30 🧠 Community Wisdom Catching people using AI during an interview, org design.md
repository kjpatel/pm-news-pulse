## Summary

This Community Wisdom edition curates the most practical advice from Lenny's subscriber Slack community on topics ranging from interviewing candidates using AI, to organizational design challenges, product naming decisions, and career transitions into product management.

## Key Takeaways

- **Screen for AI usage** during interviews by asking candidates to solve problems in real-time and observing their process, communication, and problem-solving approach rather than just final answers.
- **Document institutional knowledge** before it becomes trapped in one person's head by implementing systems, wikis, and regular knowledge-sharing sessions to reduce organizational fragility.
- **Time product renames carefully** by considering market positioning, brand equity, and user confusion costs—renaming should align with strategic shifts, not just aesthetic preferences.
- **Leverage domain expertise** when transitioning careers (e.g., nursing to health-tech PM) as your specialized knowledge becomes a competitive advantage in understanding user problems.
