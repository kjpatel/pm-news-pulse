# The Lenny and Friends Summit is back!

## Summary

Lenny Rachitsky is announcing the return of the Lenny and Friends Summit, an exclusive in-person event for senior product and AI leaders scheduled for September 10, 2026, in San Francisco, designed to foster deep learning, skill-building, and meaningful peer connections.

## Key Takeaways

- **Apply strategically** - The summit prioritizes handpicked attendees with paid newsletter subscribers getting priority consideration; applications are competitive due to limited venue capacity.
- **Target seniority level** - This event is designed for senior leaders (Chief, VP, Director, Senior, Group, Lead, or Principal titles); early-career PMs should wait for future events tailored to their level.
- **Expect high-value networking** - Previous attendees cited this as the highest-ROI networking event they've attended, emphasizing the power of IRL connection for product leaders who often lack peer support.
- **Prepare for hands-on learning** - The summit includes interactive workshops and tactical takeaways alongside talks, with attendees reporting immediate, actionable learnings they could implement.
