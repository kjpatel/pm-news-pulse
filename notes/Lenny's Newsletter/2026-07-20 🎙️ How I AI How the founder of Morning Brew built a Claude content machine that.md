## Summary

Alex Lieberman shares how he built a Claude-powered content machine that eliminates writer's block by automating idea discovery, interview extraction, voice calibration, and quality control through a systematic workflow. The system uses AI to handle the mechanical aspects of content creation while keeping the human at the center of idea generation and voice authenticity.

## Key Takeaways

- **Build an AI Oracle for idea discovery** that scans your Slack, Notion, Gmail, and social feeds to surface ranked content spikes daily—this eliminates the blank page problem that kills consistency.
- **Codify your voice in a Markdown file** that captures your top-performing posts, hook formulas, and language patterns, then use it as a constraint for all AI drafting so outputs match your actual writing style.
- **Deploy interview personas** (Tim Ferriss, Joe Rogan, etc.) that extract specifics through follow-up questions rather than relying on initial thoughts—use voice-to-text to ensure every sentence traces back to something you actually said.
- **Implement a Writer's Council scoring system** with multiple personas reviewing drafts against a 9/10 threshold before publishing, which maintains quality standards automatically and reduces manual policing.
- **Create a reinforcement loop** by comparing original drafts to published versions after each piece, extracting lessons learned, and updating a permanent lessons file so the system improves with every use.
