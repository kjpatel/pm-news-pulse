## Summary

This Community Wisdom column curates practical advice from Lenny's Slack community on topics including Claude Code accessibility for ADHD users, management techniques, career transitions from corporate to startup, and burnout from over-evangelizing AI tools.

## Key Takeaways

- **Adapt Claude Code for ADHD workflows** - Explore community-shared strategies for using Claude Code in ways that work with ADHD brains rather than against them, such as breaking tasks into smaller chunks or using specific prompts.
- **Develop upward management skills** - Learn from peer experiences on how to effectively communicate with and influence your manager, including when to escalate issues and how to frame feedback constructively.
- **Plan your corporate-to-startup transition deliberately** - Seek advice from community members who've made this jump to understand compensation changes, culture shock, and how to evaluate startup opportunities before committing.
- **Recognize and address AI evangelist burnout** - Acknowledge when promoting AI tools becomes exhausting and set boundaries around how much time you spend educating others or troubleshooting their AI adoption.
- **Leverage peer wisdom over isolated problem-solving** - Use your community network to crowdsource solutions and validate approaches rather than assuming you need to figure out complex career and technical decisions alone.
