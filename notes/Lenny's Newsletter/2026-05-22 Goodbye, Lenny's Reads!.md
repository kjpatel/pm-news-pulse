# Goodbye, Lenny's Reads!

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/goodbye-lennys-reads)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky | **Date**: May 22, 2026

---

## Summary

Lenny Rachitsky is discontinuing the Lenny's Reads audio experiment after a year, while keeping all episodes available and directing audiences to his other content channels.

## Key Takeaways

- **Archive your content** - Keep all existing episodes accessible even after discontinuing a project to maintain value for your audience
- **Redirect your audience** - Provide clear alternative channels (Twitter, LinkedIn, podcast, newsletter) where people can continue following your work
- **Communicate transparently** - Explain the decision to end an experiment directly to your audience rather than quietly discontinuing it
- **Know when to pivot** - Not every initiative needs to continue indefinitely; evaluate whether an experiment is still providing value and be willing to move on
