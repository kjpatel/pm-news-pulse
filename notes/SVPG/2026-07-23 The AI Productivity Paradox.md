# The AI Productivity Paradox

**Source**: [SVPG](https://www.svpg.com/the-ai-productivity-paradox/)
**Author**: Marty Cagan | **Date**: Jul 23, 2026

---

## Summary

While AI adoption is increasing delivery speed, most teams fail to see improved outcomes because they use AI to accelerate their old project-based workflows rather than adopting a product model that emphasizes discovery before delivery. Strong product teams differentiate themselves by using AI to validate solutions with users (build to learn) before investing in commercial-quality delivery (build to earn).

## Key Takeaways

- **Distinguish build to learn from build to earn**: Use AI to accelerate discovery and validation with users before scaling delivery, not to rush artifact creation like PRDs and roadmaps that lack customer validation.
- **Recognize the real bottleneck isn't speed**: The constraint is identifying ideas worth building, not execution velocity—AI won't solve poor strategy or weak discovery practices that lead to building solutions customers don't need.
- **Adopt a product model over a project model**: Teams seeing AI ROI have shifted from optimizing for output (faster delivery of predetermined solutions) to optimizing for outcomes (discovering what customers actually value before building).
- **Test proposed solutions with stakeholders early**: Before using AI to build commercial-quality products, invest time validating your solution addresses both customer needs and business viability through evidence-based discovery.
