# A Proactive System of Intelligence for Security

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/artemis/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Apr 14, 2026

---

## Summary

Artemis is building a next-generation SIEM platform that replaces wooden shield legacy systems with AI-powered semantic understanding, agentic detection, and closed-loop learning to defend against sophisticated AI-generated attacks. The company has already achieved dozen production enterprise deployments processing over a billion events per hour.

## Key Takeaways

- **Implement semantic understanding** to correlate user identities across systems (e.g., 'jdoe' in Okta matching 'john.doe' in AWS) and detect sequences of individually benign actions that constitute attacks.
- **Replace brittle rule-based detection** with multi-step reasoning agents that dynamically query data, perform aggregations, and reason about context before surfacing alerts.
- **Design for continuous improvement** by converting each incident or threat hunt into permanently validated detections that are autonomously maintained, ensuring your security system improves over time rather than degrading.
- **Recognize that legacy SIEM architecture is obsolete** in an era of AI-generated attacks like deepfake scams and sophisticated phishing, requiring modern database-first approaches built with AI capabilities.
