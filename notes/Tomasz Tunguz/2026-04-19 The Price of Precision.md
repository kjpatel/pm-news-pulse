# The Price of Precision

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/hidden-cost-smarter-ai/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Apr 19, 2026

---

## Summary

As AI models become smarter and more efficient, they paradoxically consume more tokens due to higher precision tokenizers, creating a sawtooth pricing pattern where efficiency gains fight against increased accuracy costs.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor your per-task costs, not per-token costs** — Opus 4.5 costs 67% more per token but only 40% of the actual price per task due to 76% fewer tokens needed, so evaluate true end-to-end economics
- **Expect tokenizer upgrades to temporarily increase expenses** — New tokenizers like Opus 4.7's provide better accuracy and fewer mistakes on coding tasks but generate ~46% more tokens, requiring budget adjustments until efficiency gains compound
- **Plan for a sawtooth pricing cycle** — Smarter models will alternate between expensive precision phases (new tokenizers) and cheaper efficiency phases (better reasoning), so build flexible token budgets rather than fixed costs
