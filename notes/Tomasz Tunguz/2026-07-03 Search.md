**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Jul 03, 2026

## Summary

The article explores how search is being fundamentally transformed by AI, shifting from traditional keyword-based queries to more intelligent, contextual, and agent-driven discovery mechanisms.

## Key Takeaways

- **Rethink search interfaces** - Move beyond the chat box paradigm to explore how AI can understand intent contextually rather than requiring precise keyword formulation
- **Integrate AI agents into discovery** - Consider autonomous agents that can proactively search and retrieve information on behalf of users rather than waiting for explicit queries
- **Optimize for semantic understanding** - Build search systems that understand meaning and context across distributed information sources instead of relying on traditional indexing
- **Design for continuous learning** - Implement search mechanisms that improve through usage patterns and user behavior rather than static algorithmic ranking
