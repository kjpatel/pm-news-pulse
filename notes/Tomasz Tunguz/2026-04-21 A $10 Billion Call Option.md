# A $10 Billion Call Option

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/spacex-cursor-10b-call-option/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Apr 21, 2026

---

## Summary

SpaceX's $10 billion partnership with Cursor and $60 billion acquisition option represents a strategic play to combine Cursor's dominant developer distribution with xAI's compute infrastructure, addressing Cursor's third-party model dependency and xAI's collapsing market share.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize the three-layer stack** required to win in agentic coding: compute, models, and distribution—and identify which layer your company owns versus depends on third parties for.
- **Monitor model volume trends** as a key competitive indicator; xAI's 90% decline in weekly token processing (6 trillion to 0.6 trillion) signals rapid market consolidation and the danger of losing mindshare.
- **Prioritize independence in critical dependencies** before acquiring distribution—Cursor's margin pressure and model vulnerability make it a strategic acquisition target despite strong user adoption.
- **Understand acquisition optionality** as a form of market insurance; SpaceX secures Cursor's distribution rights while maintaining flexibility to decide on full acquisition based on xAI model performance.
