# Founders, Equip Your Agents

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/lena-waters-office-hours/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz (featuring Lena Waters) | **Date**: Apr 10, 2026

---

## Summary

The buyer journey has fundamentally shifted as AI agents now participate in purchasing decisions, requiring founders to rethink go-to-market strategies by equipping agents with the information and tools they need rather than optimizing for human browsing behavior.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize debt repayment vs. transformation**: Most companies are simply automating broken processes through agents rather than creating new paradigms; focus on fixing foundational go-to-market issues first.
- **Redesign for agent consumption**: Rebuild websites and product documentation for AI agent parsing (markdown, structured data) rather than human psychology and visual design, as agents don't respond to styling or emotional appeals.
- **Treat agents as internal champions**: Equip AI agents with the same information, access, and decision-making support you'd provide to your best sales advocate, since they now participate in or make purchasing decisions.
- **Understand agent decision authority limits**: In enterprise deals, humans remain accountable for decisions, so agents function as research and analysis tools that report to human decision-makers rather than autonomous decision-makers.
