# Timing Cashflows of Your Sales Commissions and Your Customers

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/timing-sales-cashflows/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Apr 30, 2026

---

## Summary

Aligning the timing of sales commission payouts with customer cash inflow is critical for maintaining healthy company finances and preventing cash flow crises. Managing this mismatch can make the difference between sustainable growth and operational strain.

## Key Takeaways

- **Map your commission schedule** against actual customer payment cycles to identify cash flow gaps where you're paying commissions before receiving customer revenue.
- **Negotiate staggered commission payouts** tied to customer milestones (contract signature, first payment, revenue recognition) rather than paying commissions upfront on deals.
- **Model worst-case scenarios** where customers delay payment while sales commissions accelerate, and ensure your cash reserves can absorb these timing mismatches.
- **Communicate transparently with sales** about commission timing changes—frame them as company sustainability measures rather than compensation cuts.
