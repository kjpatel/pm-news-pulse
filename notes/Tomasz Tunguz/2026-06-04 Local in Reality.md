# Local in Reality

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/local-tokens/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Jun 04, 2026

---

## Summary

The article argues that true localization in AI systems requires physical grounding and real-world context rather than just language translation or cultural adaptation. Effective local AI must understand and operate within the specific constraints, infrastructure, and realities of each geographic market.

## Key Takeaways

- **Build for local infrastructure** - Design AI products around existing local systems, connectivity, and technical constraints rather than expecting users to adapt to your architecture.
- **Embed cultural and contextual knowledge** - Move beyond surface-level localization to deeply understand local business practices, regulatory environments, and user behaviors specific to each market.
- **Test with real local users early** - Validate assumptions about local markets through direct engagement with actual users in those regions rather than making remote decisions.
- **Create region-specific data strategies** - Develop localized datasets and training approaches that reflect local patterns, languages, and edge cases rather than applying global models uniformly.
