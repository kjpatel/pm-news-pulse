# Open Models Tack Toward the Frontier

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/open-models-tack-toward-the-frontier/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Jul 20, 2026

---

## Summary

Open-weight AI models are rapidly closing the gap with closed-source frontier models, creating a competitive dynamic where closed models drive innovation and open models quickly commoditize gains, accelerating overall industry progress.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor the commoditization cycle**: Track when open models reach parity with closed frontier models (DeepSeek R1, GLM-5.2, Kimi K3) as this signals upcoming price compression and margin pressure across the AI stack.
- **Leverage cost advantages strategically**: Open-source models like DeepSeek V4 Flash cost ~90% less than GPT-5.2, creating opportunities to optimize inference costs while closed models maintain performance leadership.
- **Expect continuous architectural innovation**: Competition is driving rapid releases of novel architectures (e.g., Kimi's KDA attention mechanism) that force the entire industry to innovate faster—use this to inform product roadmaps.
- **Plan for margin compression**: As open models commoditize capabilities, pricing power will decline; build defensibility through differentiation, not just model performance or cost leadership alone.
