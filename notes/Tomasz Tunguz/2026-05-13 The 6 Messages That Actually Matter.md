# The 6 Messages That Actually Matter

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/the-disappearance-of-email/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: May 13, 2026

---

## Summary

Email inboxes are becoming unmanageable with 121+ daily messages, but AI-powered triage systems using custom rules and personal context will reduce emails to only the 6 that truly matter, automating workflows and keeping sensitive data private.

## Key Takeaways

- **Define custom rules** that encode your priorities and relationships in natural English to automatically route and action incoming emails before you see them.
- **Automate downstream workflows** by connecting email triage to downstream tools—receipts to expense platforms, leads to CRM scoring, and proposals to outbox.
- **Build a personal context layer** by preserving your email archive as institutional memory that informs how AI handles future messages and relationship history.
- **Implement on-device processing** for sensitive messages to ensure private information stays local while automated systems handle lower-risk communication.
