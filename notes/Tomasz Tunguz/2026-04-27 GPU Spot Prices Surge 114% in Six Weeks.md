# GPU Spot Prices Surge 114% in Six Weeks

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/b200-gpu-pricing-spot-market-model-releases/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Apr 27, 2026

## Summary

GPU spot prices have surged 114% in six weeks as frontier AI model releases drive demand for NVIDIA's latest Blackwell chips, creating a lucid but opaque market with widening price spreads between providers and prior-generation hardware.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor model release cycles** as demand shocks: Major AI model launches directly correlate with B200 GPU price spikes, so tracking frontier model releases helps predict infrastructure cost changes.
- **Expect continued pricing power** for cloud providers: The spot market spreads have recovered to near-launch levels, signaling a return to sellers' market conditions after six months of margin compression.
- **Plan infrastructure costs 90 days ahead**: B200 spot prices lead contract pricing by approximately three months, so AI startups should budget for B200 likely settling above $5.00/hour for summer 2026.
- **Recognize chip depreciation signals**: The $1.80 price gap between B200 and H200 indicates older chips lose value when new models demand newer architectures, affecting long-term hardware investment decisions.
