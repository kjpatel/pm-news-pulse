# The First Derivative of Inference

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/first-derivative-of-inference/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: May 15, 2026

---

## Summary

The fastest-growing AI and software companies either sell inference directly or benefit as first derivatives of inference, with companies like Datadog and Twilio seeing explosive growth by positioning themselves as essential infrastructure for AI systems. For legacy software companies facing the "Saaspocalypse," the strategic imperative is to either resell inference or fundamentally restructure around customer inference consumption.

## Key Takeaways

- **Position as inference infrastructure**: Audit your product to determine if you can resell inference or become essential infrastructure (like Twilio's voice/SMS or Datadog's observability) for companies building AI systems.
- **Expect power law concentration**: Accept that a small percentage of customers will drive the majority of ARR as they consume massive volumes of inference—design pricing and GTM accordingly.
- **Raise the board-level question now**: Make inference reselling or inference-dependent business models a strategic priority at the governance level rather than treating AI as a feature initiative.
- **Benchmark against first derivative winners**: Study how Datadog (3x QoQ growth in LLM observability) and Twilio (AI as platform entry point) captured inference-driven growth to understand positioning opportunities in your category.
