# What Would AI Email Cost?

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/cost-of-ai-email/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: May 14, 2026

---

## Summary

AI-powered email products will cost $350-500 annually using state-of-the-art models, but strategic optimization through local model execution and deterministic components can reduce costs by 100x, reshaping SaaS pricing over the next 12-24 months.

## Key Takeaways

- **Calculate true AI costs transparently**: State-of-the-art model inference for email costs $22-130/month; apply standard SaaS margins (75%) to determine realistic pricing ($500/year for agentic email solutions).
- **Optimize inference architecture strategically**: Implement local GPU execution and smaller models to reduce per-user costs by 10-20x, making AI features economically viable at scale.
- **Segment workloads by execution method**: Separate deterministic rule-based components (email filters) from model-dependent tasks to achieve 100x cost reductions through deliberate system design.
- **Prepare for GPU-constrained software design**: The upcoming 12-24 months will see winners emerge by matching lightweight models to specific workloads rather than defaulting to state-of-the-art inference.
