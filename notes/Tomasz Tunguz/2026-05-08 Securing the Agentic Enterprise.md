# Securing the Agentic Enterprise

**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz (featuring Jonathan Jaffe) | **Date**: May 08, 2026

## Summary

As enterprises increasingly rely on AI agents for operations, security must evolve to match—requiring AI-powered defenders to protect against AI-powered attackers operating at machine speed rather than human speed.

## Key Takeaways

- **Design AI defenders proactively** – Traditional security playbooks built for human adversaries are obsolete; enterprises must architect AI-powered defensive agents from the ground up to match attacker capabilities.
- **Treat agentic security as a systems problem** – Moving beyond isolated security tools, build integrated systems that monitor and operate AI agents holistically across the enterprise infrastructure.
- **Establish machine-speed oversight frameworks** – Define clear boundaries between autonomous agent responses and human judgment gates, recognizing that adversaries operate in milliseconds, not hours or days.
