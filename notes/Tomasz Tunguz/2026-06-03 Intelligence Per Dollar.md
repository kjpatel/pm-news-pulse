# Intelligence Per Dollar

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/tokens-per-result/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Jun 03, 2026

---

## Summary

AI model pricing is shifting from pure performance metrics to efficiency metrics like 'intelligence per dollar,' fundamentally changing how companies evaluate and compete on AI solutions across the entire stack.

## Key Takeaways

- **Track intelligence per dollar**: Move beyond raw benchmark scores to measure AI performance relative to cost—Microsoft's new metric of average token usage per task is becoming the industry standard for model evaluation.
- **Plan for realistic AI budgets**: Major companies like Uber and Salesforce are hitting budget caps within months of deploying AI at scale, so model aggressive token spending forecasts and implement spending controls early.
- **Price outcomes, not tokens**: Shift your competitive positioning from tokens to results—applications must compete on dollars per outcome (closed tickets, shipped PRs, resolved cases) rather than cost per token or raw intelligence.
