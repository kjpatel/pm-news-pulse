# The AI Problem Matrix: Where the Largest Opportunity Hides

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/2026-04-08-ai-problems-2x2-matrix/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: Apr 08, 2026

---

## Summary

AI businesses fall into four quadrants based on whether they operate in closed or open loops and face finite or infinite demand; the largest opportunity lies in open loop systems with infinite demand like content creation, where validation is fuzzy but market size is unlimited.

## Key Takeaways

- **Map your AI product** across the closed/open loop and finite/infinite demand axes to understand your market size ceiling and competitive defensibility.
- **Pursue open loop infinite demand** opportunities like content, research, and ideation where demand is insatiable and margins are higher due to quality differentiation.
- **Shift success metrics** from binary validation (tests pass/fail) to fuzzy metrics like engagement and influence when operating in open loop systems.
- **Build defensibility through judgment** by focusing on taste and domain expertise rather than commodity solutions, especially in content and creative work.
