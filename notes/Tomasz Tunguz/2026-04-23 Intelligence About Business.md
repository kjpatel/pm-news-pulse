# Intelligence About Business

## Summary

AI is fundamentally transforming business intelligence from static dashboards to dynamic, conversational systems that analyze both structured and unstructured data to provide actionable business insights. The future of BI lies in semantic models that enable AI to understand company knowledge across multiple data sources.

## Key Takeaways

- **Expand BI beyond dashboards** by implementing AI systems that can analyze unstructured data (support logs, conversations, documents) alongside structured data to uncover patterns and recommendations humans might miss
- **Build semantic models as your data foundation** that store definitions, logic, and permissions across all data sources, enabling both traditional dashboards and AI-powered natural language queries
- **Use AI to automate data-dependent workflows** like bug intake and analytics review—let AI systems search multiple sources, identify existing issues, and draft structured outputs that require only final user approval
- **Measure BI success by business outcomes, not dashboard completion**—track how many support rep mistakes were identified, how many duplicate bug reports were prevented, or how much analyst time was freed up
