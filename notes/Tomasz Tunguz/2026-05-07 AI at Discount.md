# AI at Discount

**Source**: [Tomasz Tunguz](https://www.tomtunguz.com/ai-at-discount/)
**Author**: Tomasz Tunguz | **Date**: May 07, 2026

---

## Summary

Anthropic trades at a significant 65% discount to comparable companies despite growing 3x faster than Palantir, driven by capital intensity, profitability uncertainty, growth volatility, and political risk in the rapidly evolving AI market.

## Key Takeaways

- **Analyze capital requirements carefully** - AI infrastructure companies face massive upfront costs (e.g., Anthropic's $6.2B annual GPU spending), which creates valuation uncertainty for public markets and justifies lower multiples despite high growth.
- **Monitor profitability timeline** - The gap between revenue multiples and actual valuations reflects uncertainty about whether AI companies will achieve high-margin software business status or remain capital-intensive utilities; clarify your path to profitability early.
- **Prepare for regulatory volatility** - Export controls, compute caps, and safety requirements could rapidly reshape competitive dynamics; build contingency plans for different regulatory scenarios rather than assuming current trajectories.
- **Understand growth predictability** - Public markets discount explosive but volatile growth (like Anthropic's March-April spike); demonstrating consistent, understandable growth curves improves valuation multiples regardless of absolute growth rate.
- **Price in exogenous risks** - The 65% discount is rational given market uncertainty; use this as a framework for valuing other early-stage infrastructure companies in fast-changing markets.
