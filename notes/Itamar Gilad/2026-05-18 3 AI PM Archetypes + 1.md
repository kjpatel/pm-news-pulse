# 3 AI PM Archetypes + 1

**Source**: [Itamar Gilad](https://itamargilad.com/ai-pm-future/)
**Author**: Itamar Gilad | **Date**: May 18, 2026

---

## Summary

The article examines four product management archetypes in the AI era: the AI PM (deep technical AI knowledge), the Developer PM (using coding tools), the No PM scenario, and the overlooked AI-Empowered PM who leverages AI across all eight company functions to drive modernization and competitive advantage.

## Key Takeaways

- **Resist specializing exclusively in AI skills** — Only 1-in-7 PM roles require deep AI expertise; instead develop business and market knowledge while building basic AI literacy like other specialized domains.
- **Adopt AI-native tools strategically** — Use coding environments like Claude Code and Cowork for context management and cross-file searching, but recognize these are stopgaps until PM-specific tools mature.
- **Move upstream from delivery work** — Pure delivery product owners translating roadmaps into specs are most vulnerable to AI replacement; transition toward strategic roadmap creation and stakeholder alignment work.
- **Build the AI-Empowered PM archetype** — Apply AI across all eight company functions (strategy, discovery, execution, operations, etc.) rather than just feature delivery to create sustainable competitive advantages.
- **Modernize your company operating model holistically** — Companies that apply AI across all organizational functions rather than just doubling down on feature factories will outcompete those treating AI as an execution accelerator.
