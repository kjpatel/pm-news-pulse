# Standard Intelligence: Training General Intelligence in Pixel Space

**Source**: [Sequoia Capital](https://sequoiacap.com/article/standard-intelligence-training-general-intelligence-in-pixel-space/)
**Author**: Sequoia Capital | **Date**: Apr 30, 2026

---

## Summary

Standard Intelligence is pioneering a novel approach to building general computer agents by training directly on video data of human computer use rather than relying on language models and tool calls. Their pixel-based pre-training paradigm, demonstrated through their FDM-1 foundation model, represents a fundamentally different architectural bet on scaling agents.

## Key Takeaways

- **Adopt pixel-first thinking**: Consider training agents directly on raw screen video and action sequences rather than wrapping language models in increasingly complex tool-calling harnesses, as this may be more scalable for general computer automation.
- **Build efficient infrastructure from first principles**: Standard Intelligence achieved 50× token efficiency in video encoding and 20× cost savings on storage by reasoning through technical challenges without inherited assumptions, suggesting significant gains are possible through fundamental rethinking.
- **Embrace contrarian technical bets with conviction**: The most promising path to general agents may require rejecting conventional wisdom about language-first approaches and investing in unconventional paradigms that can truly scale action data.
- **Combine safety consciousness with technical ambition**: The most impactful AI builders are those who are both urgently mission-driven on capabilities and deeply serious about safety and alignment from the start.
