# AI Ascent 2026

**Source**: [Sequoia Capital](https://sequoiacap.com/article/ai-ascent-2026/)
**Author**: Sequoia Capital | **Date**: May 08, 2026

---

## Summary

AI Ascent 2026 brought together 150+ leading AI founders and researchers to discuss how AI represents a computational revolution with practical applications, emphasizing that founders should build moats from customers backward, design for affordance, and capitalize on the gap between model capabilities and enterprise deployment.

## Key Takeaways

- **Build moats from the customer back** - Rather than starting with model capabilities, design products around real customer problems and work backward to determine what technical capabilities you actually need.
- **Design for affordance** - Create interfaces and experiences that make AI capabilities naturally usable and intuitive for end users, not just technically impressive.
- **Exploit the diffusion gap** - Capture value by addressing the significant lag between cutting-edge model capabilities and what Fortune 500 companies have actually deployed in production.
- **Recognize agents as the 2026 inflection point** - The convergence of three key ingredients (models, tools, and harnesses) has made agent-based applications viable and represents the next major wave of AI product innovation.
