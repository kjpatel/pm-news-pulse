# Listen to the Market

**Source**: [Sequoia Capital](https://sequoiacap.com/article/listen-to-the-market/)
**Author**: Sequoia Capital (featuring Alfred Lin and Abhishek Malani) | **Date**: Jun 01, 2026

---

## Summary

Kalshi's new American Power Index (KPOW) uses prediction markets to provide a continuous, real-time measure of political power shifts in Washington, offering clearer insight than traditional polls, media, or punditry by forcing market participants to back beliefs with capital.

## Key Takeaways

- **Use market prices as a signal processor** - Markets force people to back beliefs with actual capital, creating a more reliable truth-seeking mechanism than commentary, polls, or algorithms that amplify narrow viewpoints.
- **Convert vague arguments into testable measurements** - Track how indices move in response to specific events (court rulings, primary upsets, policy shocks) to objectively measure impact rather than debate endlessly about influence.
- **Build infrastructure before scaling use cases** - Kalshi spent years building liquidity, regulation, and settlement infrastructure that now enables new instruments beyond traditional prediction markets to address never-settling questions.
- **Design for continuous measurement of ongoing questions** - Unlike traditional prediction markets that require a finish line, an index approach allows pricing of complex, ongoing questions (like power tilting) by returning a continuously updated number rather than a binary winner.
