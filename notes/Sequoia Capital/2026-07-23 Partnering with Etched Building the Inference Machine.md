# Partnering with Etched: Building the Inference Machine

**Source**: [Sequoia Capital](https://sequoiacap.com/article/partnering-with-etched-building-the-inference-machine/)
**Author**: Sequoia Capital (featuring Sonya Huang and Abhishek Malani) | **Date**: Jul 23, 2026

---

## Summary

Sequoia Capital is partnering with Etched, a hardware startup building custom silicon specifically optimized for AI inference at scale, leading a $300M Series C as inference emerges as potentially the largest market in AI.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prioritize cluster-scale design**: Build hardware systems optimized for rack and cluster-level performance rather than individual chips, as the unit of compute has fundamentally shifted from chip to cluster architecture.
- **Ship production-ready hardware**: Focus on getting working systems into customer hands quickly—Etched brought their first cluster to production in just 40 days after tape-out, demonstrating that execution and shipping matter more than perfection.
- **Iterate faster than model evolution**: Match the pace of AI model changes (weeks between releases, shifting architectures, expanding context lengths) by building teams young and agile enough to push hardware frontiers while staying synchronized with software.
- **Design for the full spectrum of architectures**: Create inference systems that handle diverse model types—sparse MoEs, dense transformers, and alternative architectures like Mamba—rather than optimizing for a single approach.
