# All Systems Nominal – Nominal Spotlight

**Source**: [Sequoia Capital](https://sequoiacap.com/article/all-systems-nominal-nominal-spotlight/)
**Author**: Sequoia Capital | **Date**: May 21, 2026

---

## Summary

Cameron McCord, a former Navy submarine officer, founded Nominal to revolutionize hardware testing and deployment by bringing real-time data analysis to complex systems, enabling companies like Hermeus to conduct critical flight tests in hours instead of weeks.

## Key Takeaways

- **Identify your unique pain point** – McCord spent years observing the military and aerospace industry's reliance on outdated, patchwork data review tools before creating a focused solution for hardware testing.
- **Translate domain expertise into products** – His deep understanding of submarine systems, mission-critical decision-making, and hardware complexity directly informed Nominal's design for the aerospace and defense sectors.
- **Enable faster iteration cycles** – By compressing data analysis from weeks/months to hours, Nominal allows teams like Hermeus to take calculated risks and move from preliminary testing to high-stakes deployments in a single day.
- **Build trust through competence and service** – McCord's leadership philosophy of earning respect through understanding complexity and commitment to those relying on you became the foundation for how Nominal serves its customers.
- **Recognize when to pivot your impact model** – Understanding that traditional paths (like military careers) may not align with your unique skill set allows you to create outsized impact in adjacent fields.
