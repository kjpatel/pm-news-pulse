# Partnering with Ineffable Intelligence: A Superlearner for the Era of Experience

**Source**: [Sequoia Capital](https://sequoiacap.com/article/partnering-with-ineffable-intelligence-a-superlearner-for-the-era-of-experience/)
**Author**: Sequoia Capital (featuring David Silver) | **Date**: Apr 27, 2026

---

## Summary

Sequoia Capital is partnering with David Silver's new AI research lab, Ineffable Intelligence, to develop a reinforcement learning-based 'superlearner' that discovers knowledge through pure experience rather than human data pre-training, potentially enabling AI systems to derive and transcend fundamental human discoveries.

## Key Takeaways

- **Back fundamental paradigm shifts**: Ineffable's RL-first approach deliberately rejects the current LLM-dominant consensus by building systems from pure experience and self-play, echoing how AlphaGo Zero surpassed human-trained AlphaGo by removing pre-training entirely.
- **Recognize non-human reasoning advantages**: A superlearner trained without human data constraints may develop novel problem-solving strategies for domains humans haven't yet conceptualized, from inventing new mathematical branches to discovering materials with no existing vocabulary.
- **Invest in contrarian technical depth**: The partnership prioritizes founders like David Silver who have repeatedly ignored consensus correctly—his track record scaling self-play from Go to AlphaZero demonstrates the conviction and technical execution needed for moonshot bets.
- **Design learning systems for emergence**: Rather than shortcutting with human internet data, build agents in environments that force discovering fundamental principles from first principles, enabling genuine superintelligence rather than sophisticated mimicry.
