## Summary

This week's Stratechery newsletter covers IBM's historic stock collapse driven by weakening mainframe sales as AI threatens to port legacy systems, OpenAI's expanding ambitions through new hardware and app redesigns, and Netflix's declining dominance as it struggles to maintain premium positioning.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor legacy technology disruption**: AI's ability to port essential backend programs from archaic mainframe technology represents a permanent threat to IBM's customer retention, not just temporary sales weakness.
- **Track OpenAI's hardware expansion**: The company's new ambient speaker with robotic components signals a strategic shift beyond software toward becoming a complete hardware-software ecosystem competitor.
- **Reassess premium platform positioning**: Netflix's pivot toward YouTube-mimicking content and audience retention tactics suggests premium streaming platforms face existential challenges when original content becomes commoditized.
