# 2026.23: Power Shifts

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/power-shifts/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: Jun 05, 2026

---

## Summary

Power is shifting in tech from traditional gatekeepers to new platforms and creators, as evidenced by YouTubers entering Hollywood, changes in search dominance between Google and Microsoft, and broader market realignments in the AI era.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor platform shifts** - Track when distribution channels change hands (YouTubers vs traditional studios, Bing vs Google) as these represent major market reorientations that affect competitive positioning
- **Understand gatekeeper erosion** - Traditional content gatekeepers losing power to creators and new platforms signals that your competitive moat may be vulnerable if you rely on incumbency rather than product quality
- **Map power dynamics quarterly** - Establish a regular cadence to assess who controls distribution, discovery, and audience attention in your market, as these power holders determine success
