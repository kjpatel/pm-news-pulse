# An Interview with Parallel Founder Parag Agarwal About Valuing Content on the Agentic Web

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/an-interview-with-parallel-founder-parag-agarwal-about-valuing-content-on-the-agentic-web/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson (featuring Parag Agarwal) | **Date**: May 21, 2026

---

## Summary

Parag Agarwal, founder of Parallel, discusses how content creators can be fairly valued and compensated in an agentic web where AI agents consume content at scale. The interview explores mechanisms for incentivizing quality content creation in a world where traditional distribution models are disrupted by agent-driven consumption.

## Key Takeaways

- **Align incentives** by creating economic models that fairly compensate content creators when their work is consumed by AI agents at scale, moving beyond traditional ad-supported or subscription models.
- **Design for agent consumption** by building content platforms that account for how AI agents will discover, evaluate, and utilize content differently than human users.
- **Establish content valuation frameworks** that can dynamically price and attribute value to content based on its utility to agents, rather than relying on legacy metrics tied to human attention.
- **Rethink distribution models** for creators in an agentic era where content consumption patterns will be fundamentally different, requiring new approaches to reach and monetize audiences.
