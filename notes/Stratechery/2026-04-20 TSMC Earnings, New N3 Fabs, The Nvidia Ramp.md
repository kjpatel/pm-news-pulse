## Summary

TSMC's earnings results and capital allocation decisions suggest the company's leadership harbors skepticism about the sustainability of AI-driven growth, despite market enthusiasm for AI chip demand.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor capital allocation as a signal:** Analyze where executives are investing (or not investing) as a leading indicator of their true conviction in growth narratives, which often contradicts public messaging.
- **Question consensus narratives:** When market consensus assumes unlimited AI demand will drive chip capacity utilization, examine management behavior for signs of doubt and adjust your assumptions accordingly.
- **Track fab expansion timelines:** Watch new N3 fab announcements and their production ramp schedules as crucial data points to understand actual capacity constraints versus theoretical demand.
