# 2026.28: XBOX On the Rocks

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/xbox-on-the-rocks/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: Jul 10, 2026

---

## Summary

Microsoft's Xbox division is undergoing massive layoffs (3,200 jobs, ~20% of staff) due to the catastrophic failure of its Game Pass strategy, illustrating broader failures in internet economics and management decisions that have plagued Xbox for years.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize bundling failures early** – Game Pass's inability to generate sustainable revenue despite massive investments reveals how bundling strategies can destroy value when underlying unit economics don't work.
- **Audit sunk cost decisions regularly** – Microsoft continued pouring resources into Xbox and Game Pass for years despite mounting evidence of failure; establish clear metrics and kill-switch criteria before committing to major initiatives.
- **Understand internet economics constraints** – Xbox's model conflicted with fundamental internet economics principles; validate that your business model aligns with how digital markets actually function before scaling.
