# 2026.21: The Data Center Veto

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/the-data-center-veto/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: May 22, 2026

---

## Summary

Data centers, which are essential physical infrastructure for AI, face mounting local opposition, giving ordinary citizens unprecedented veto power over AI development—a dynamic more important than addressing misinformation about data centers.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize the physical constraint**: AI's digital impact is paired with dependence on physical data centers, creating a new point of leverage for communities that globalization never provided.
- **Address root causes, not symptoms**: Community opposition to data centers stems from legitimate concerns; focusing on correcting misinformation misses the underlying economic and environmental issues driving resistance.
- **Develop compensation frameworks**: The only viable solution is structuring financial incentives that fairly compensate local communities for hosting data center infrastructure.
- **Monitor emerging business models**: The shift toward agentic web traffic will fundamentally alter internet economics, making content valuation and ad-supported models obsolete—requiring new approaches to incentivizing creation.
