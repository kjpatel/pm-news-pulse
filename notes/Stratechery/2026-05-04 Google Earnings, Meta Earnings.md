# Google Earnings, Meta Earnings

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/google-earnings-meta-earnings/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: May 04, 2026

---

## Summary

Google's earnings were celebrated by Wall Street despite Meta demonstrating stronger core business performance, largely because Google is successfully monetizing its AI investments while Meta lags in capturing value from its technology.

## Key Takeaways

- **Track monetization timing**: Google's ability to convert AI investments into near-term revenue gains market favor more than superior underlying metrics; focus on when value realization occurs, not just when value is created.
- **Anthropic partnership impact**: Google's earnings success may primarily reflect its Anthropic investment monetization, suggesting AI model provider relationships are now critical to revenue growth narratives.
- **Decouple metrics from valuation**: Don't assume strong operational metrics (like Meta's core business performance) automatically translate to positive market reception; investor perception of monetization strategy matters equally.
