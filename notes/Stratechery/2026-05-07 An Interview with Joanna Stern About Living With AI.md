## Summary

Ben Thompson interviews journalist Joanna Stern about her new book on living with AI and her launch of a new media company, exploring practical experiences and lessons from integrating AI into daily life.

## Key Takeaways

- **Document your AI integration journey** - Create a narrative around how AI tools are reshaping your work and life to help others understand practical applications beyond hype
- **Build media properties around AI literacy** - There's significant audience demand for accessible, experiential content about AI adoption written by trusted voices with credibility
- **Balance skepticism with exploration** - Approach AI tools with critical evaluation rather than blind adoption or dismissal to discover genuinely useful applications
