# An Interview with Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella About Finding Core Competencies

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/an-interview-with-microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-about-finding-core-competencies/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: Jun 04, 2026

---

## Summary

Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella discusses the company's strategic positioning in the AI era, emphasizing that Microsoft's core competency lies in building trusted platforms that enable others to create value rather than competing directly with frontier models. He articulates a vision of a multi-tenant learning system where organizations develop their own specialized AI capabilities.

## Key Takeaways

- **Focus on platform strategy over model competition**: Identify what your company is uniquely capable of building rather than attempting to compete in every frontier—Microsoft's strength is enabling others to build on its platform, not just creating frontier models.
- **Develop specialized, domain-specific AI models**: Build models from scratch with clean lineage tailored to specific business needs rather than distilling from general-purpose frontier models, allowing for better performance in particular use cases.
- **Establish multi-tenant learning systems**: Create infrastructure that allows organizations to develop their own proprietary AI capabilities ('hill-climbing machines') as a core business value proposition.
- **Recognize infrastructure as a critical moat**: Invest heavily in datacenter capacity and computational infrastructure—the ability to serve models at scale with reliable, trustworthy operations becomes a sustainable competitive advantage.
