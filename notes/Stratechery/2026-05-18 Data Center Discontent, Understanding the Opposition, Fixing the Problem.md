# Data Center Discontent, Understanding the Opposition, Fixing the Problem

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/data-center-discontent-understanding-the-opposition-fixing-the-problem/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: May 18, 2026

## Summary

Data center expansion faces legitimate community opposition, but the only viable solution is for companies to directly compensate affected communities rather than attempting to address all concerns.

## Key Takeaways

- **Acknowledge legitimate concerns**: Community opposition to data centers stems from understandable issues like environmental impact, land use, and local disruption—not merely NIMBYism or irrationality.
- **Direct compensation works**: Rather than trying to win hearts and minds or address every objection, the most effective approach is offering meaningful financial incentives to local communities.
- **Stop defensive messaging**: Companies attempting to counter opposition through education or advocacy campaigns have limited effectiveness compared to economic solutions.
