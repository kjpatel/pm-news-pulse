## Summary

This week's Stratechery bundle explores how opportunity costs (rather than marginal costs) are reshaping AI economics, Amazon's strategic satellite acquisition to compete with SpaceX, and lessons from F1 champion Nico Rosberg's transition to venture capital.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prioritize opportunity cost analysis** in AI infrastructure decisions—with compute scarcity intensifying, focus on which projects generate the highest ROI rather than minimizing marginal electricity costs.
- **Look beyond surface-level competitive narratives**—Amazon's Globalstar deal may primarily serve Apple's connectivity needs rather than direct Starlink competition, revealing hidden strategic partnerships.
- **Study adjacent domain expertise for investment insight**—Rosberg's F1 discipline around maximizing competitive edges and understanding personal advantages applies directly to venture capital strategy and startup evaluation.
