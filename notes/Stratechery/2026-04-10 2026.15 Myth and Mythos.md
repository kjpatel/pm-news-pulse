## Summary

Anthropic has emerged as the dominant AI company with a powerful new model, though skepticism about safety claims and questions around media narratives about tech leaders like Sam Altman reveal deeper issues in how the AI industry is covered and understood.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize exponential trajectories early** - Anthropic's success didn't happen overnight; the company has been on an accelerating growth curve for nearly two years that many observers missed, illustrating the importance of tracking compound progress rather than just recent headlines.
- **Evaluate safety claims with context** - When companies claim models are too dangerous to release publicly, consider both legitimate safety concerns and potential strategic motivations; historical precedent matters but doesn't guarantee future accuracy.
- **Demand substantive tech coverage** - Much tech journalism settles for biographical narratives and predetermined conclusions instead of exploring the dramatic, consequential questions that actually matter about industry leaders and their decisions.
