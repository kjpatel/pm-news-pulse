## Summary

This is a Stratechery Plus subscriber-only interview with Ben Thompson discussing the implications of compute shortages on Aggregation Theory, consumer AI, and related tech industry dynamics.

## Key Takeaways

- **Understand compute constraints** - The compute shortage is reshaping how technology companies build products and compete, making it essential to understand its ripple effects across the industry
- **Revisit Aggregation Theory** - Traditional aggregation models may need reassessment given new constraints, requiring founders and strategists to reconsider competitive advantages
- **Monitor consumer AI evolution** - The intersection of compute limitations and consumer AI adoption will determine which products succeed and which fail in the coming years
