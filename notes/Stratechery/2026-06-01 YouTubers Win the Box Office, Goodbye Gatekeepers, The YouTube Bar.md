# YouTubers Win the Box Office, Goodbye Gatekeepers, The YouTube Bar

**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: Jun 01, 2026

## Summary

YouTubers are succeeding at the box office because the bar for success on YouTube—direct audience engagement and merit-based performance—is higher than traditional Hollywood gatekeeping mechanisms that determine studio backing.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize that platform success metrics are more demanding than traditional gatekeeping**: YouTube's direct audience feedback and algorithmic requirements create a higher bar for success than studio decisions based on relationships or conventional wisdom.
- **Understand distribution moats are shifting from institutional control to audience relationship building**: The gatekeepers' power is diminishing as creators with proven YouTube audiences can directly access audiences for film releases.
- **Build credibility through direct audience engagement rather than institutional validation**: Success in emerging entertainment models requires proving yourself to end users first, not securing approval from traditional intermediaries.
