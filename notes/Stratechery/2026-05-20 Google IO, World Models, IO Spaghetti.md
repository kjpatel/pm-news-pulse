## Summary

Google I/O 2026 showcased AI integration across Google's product ecosystem, but the strategy raises questions about whether DeepMind's research objectives align with Google's commercial business priorities.

## Key Takeaways

- **Assess integration strategy**: Evaluate whether your AI features are being added strategically to solve user problems or reactively to maintain competitive parity with other tech platforms.
- **Align incentives across teams**: Ensure that your research, product, and business teams have shared objectives; misalignment between technical innovation and commercial goals can dilute product value.
- **Prioritize focused implementation**: Rather than spreading AI capabilities thinly across many products, identify the highest-impact use cases where AI creates genuine differentiation and user value.
