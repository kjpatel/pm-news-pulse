**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: May 08, 2026

## Summary

Big Tech's Q1 earnings reveal massive AI capital expenditures across megacaps with divergent market reactions, while companies like Google are beginning to monetize AI investments and Amazon demonstrates durability through long-term infrastructure strategy.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor monetization timing**: Google's earnings outperformed Meta's despite similar core business strength, suggesting the market rewards companies showing clear AI ROI—track when your AI investments begin generating measurable revenue impact.
- **Understand infrastructure moats**: Amazon's long-term infrastructure investments position it well for the inference era even if it appeared behind in training—build durable foundational capabilities that compound over time rather than chasing quarterly trends.
- **Watch CapEx commitment levels**: Q1 CapEx exceeded three times Manhattan Project spending with no signs of slowing—prepare your organization for sustained massive investment cycles in AI infrastructure that will define competitive positioning for years.
