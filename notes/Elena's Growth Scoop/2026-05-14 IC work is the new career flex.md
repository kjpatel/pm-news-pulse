# IC work is the new career flex

**Source**: [Elena's Growth Scoop](https://www.elenaverna.com/p/ic-work-is-the-new-career-flex)
**Author**: Elena Verna | **Date**: May 14, 2026

---

## Summary

Individual Contributor roles are becoming the new career aspiration as AI enables single performers to complete full end-to-end projects that previously required entire teams, while maintaining senior-level compensation and impact.

## Key Takeaways

- **Redefine impact metrics** - Stop measuring career success by headcount managed and instead focus on business outcomes delivered. High-Impact ICs should be evaluated on revenue impact or cost reduction, not team size.
- **Leverage AI as a multiplier** - Use AI's 'average intelligence' to become autonomous across multiple disciplines (design, marketing, engineering) and complete projects end-to-end without cross-functional dependencies.
- **Flatten your organization structure** - Consider creating parallel IC roles instead of traditional reporting hierarchies. This reduces coordination costs and allows talented individuals to move quickly between high-leverage projects.
- **Reclaim hands-on work** - Senior professionals should maintain the ability to contribute directly to work (coding, designing, building) rather than being forced into pure coordination roles that drain fulfillment.
- **Optimize for autonomy over status** - Evaluate career moves based on the ability to drive independent impact and control your own schedule, rather than pursuing titles and team size as status signals.
