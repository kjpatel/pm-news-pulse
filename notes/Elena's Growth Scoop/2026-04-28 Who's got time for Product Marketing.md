# Who's got time for Product Marketing?

**Source**: [Elena's Growth Scoop](https://www.elenaverna.com/p/whos-got-time-for-product-marketing)
**Author**: Elena Verna (featuring Gemma Casey-Swift) | **Date**: Apr 28, 2026

---

## Summary

Product marketing has fundamentally transformed in the AI era—shifting from a bottleneck role to an enabler of speed by empowering teams to launch independently, grounding strategy in real data, and maintaining constant customer dialogue.

## Key Takeaways

- **Tier your launches** by complexity level to prevent PMM from becoming a bottleneck; enable product and engineering teams to self-serve on lower-tier launches while PMM focuses on big moments.
- **Build your ICP from actual data**, not assumptions—pull from product usage, CRM, surveys, interviews, and AI-powered insights; review weekly instead of annually to catch shifting segments early.
- **Talk to customers constantly** as a PMM function, not just at project kickoff; ask consistent questions about user intent, alternatives considered, and decision moments to fuel faster iteration.
- **Package insights for organizational alignment**—refresh core messaging and talk tracks weekly; distribute ICP updates through Slack, all-hands, and 1:1s so sales, product, and marketing move together.
- **Let PMMs build satellite products**—because they understand customers deeply, they're uniquely positioned to create assets like academies or tools that PMMs historically wouldn't have built.
