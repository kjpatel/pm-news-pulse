# The biggest takeaway from my Stripe Sessions talk

**Source**: [Elena's Growth Scoop](https://www.elenaverna.com/p/the-biggest-takeaway-from-my-stripe)
**Author**: Elena Verna | **Date**: May 05, 2026

---

## Summary

Companies systematically underinvest in monetization as a growth lever, treating pricing as policy rather than product. Elena shares lessons from Lovable's rapid experimentation with pricing, packaging, and freemium strategies that drove growth and improved customer experience.

## Key Takeaways

- **Normalize pricing iteration** – Change pricing and packaging regularly (10+ times in Lovable's first year) so customers expect evolution; static pricing actually trains customers to resist future changes
- **Invest strategically in freemium** – Treat freemium as a growth driver, not a cost center; allocate best product experiences to free tier and measure as strategic investment rather than support burden
- **Ungated AI features initially** – Place AI features lower in pricing tiers to let customers experience value before asking them to pay; monetize resulting productivity and outcomes, not model costs
- **Embrace flexible monetization models** – Layer top-ups alongside subscriptions to match actual customer usage patterns; avoid forcing rigid subscription models that create friction
- **Assign clear monetization ownership** – Designate a single owner for pricing strategy and experimentation; treat monetization changes with product-level rigor and speed rather than multi-month approval processes
