## Summary

The article provides a comprehensive comparison of AI prototyping tools (Bolt, v0, Lovable, Replit) through real-world bakeoffs and expert workflows, while announcing the addition of Bolt.new to the author's product bundle.

## Key Takeaways

- **Run real bakeoffs** - Test AI prototyping tools on your actual use cases (portfolio building, feature additions) rather than relying on marketing claims, as tool strengths vary significantly by workflow type.
- **Choose by workflow, not hype** - Select tools based on your specific needs: Bolt for speed and full-stack iteration, v0 for front-end polish with Vercel integration, Lovable for non-technical PMs, or Replit for internal tools with databases.
- **Leverage existing infrastructure first** - Before adopting new prototyping tools, check if you already have access through Google Workspace (Firebase Studio), Microsoft, or Figma (Figma Make) to minimize procurement friction.
- **Bundle annual subscriptions for cost efficiency** - Annual subscribers to the author's bundle get access to 9+ tools (including Bolt, Magic Patterns, Dovetail, etc.) for $150/year versus $28,000+ retail value.
- **Master 5 expert workflows** - The article identifies 5 specific workflows that maximize any prototyping tool's effectiveness, along with 5 common mistakes that turn good tools into poor outputs.
