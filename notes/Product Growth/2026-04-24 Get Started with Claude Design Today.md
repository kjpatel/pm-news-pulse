## Summary

Anthropic's former Chief Product Officer recently resigned from Figma's board, highlighting potential conflicts of interest or governance considerations between the two major AI and design companies.

## Key Takeaways

- **Monitor board dynamics** between AI companies and design platforms, as strategic relationships can create conflicts requiring leadership transitions.
- **Understand governance implications** when executives hold multiple leadership positions across competing or adjacent technology ecosystems.
- **Track leadership changes** at major tech companies as indicators of shifting priorities, partnerships, or strategic directions.
