# Product Design Questions Are Dead. Here's What Replaced Them.

**Source**: [Product Growth](https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/ai-system-design-interview-your)
**Author**: Aakash Gupta | **Date**: Apr 15, 2026

---

## Summary

Product design interview questions are obsolete at top AI companies—they've been replaced by AI system design interviews that evaluate technical fluency, architecture thinking, and trade-off articulation. The article provides the first comprehensive PM guide for this new interview format.

## Key Takeaways

- **Shift your interview preparation ratio** from 30-40% product framing to 60-70% system architecture focus, spending most of your time on data flows, model selection, and agent orchestration rather than user personas.
- **Master technical fluency fundamentals** by learning when to use LLMs versus traditional ML models, understanding metrics like F1 scores, and being able to articulate trade-offs between accuracy, latency, and deployment approaches.
- **Structure answers with the DASME framework** that includes data pipelines, architecture diagrams, model selection trade-offs, orchestration layers, and failure modes—not just vague "AI layers" appended to product solutions.
- **Prepare for company-specific variations** since top AI companies like OpenAI, Google, and Meta ask this question differently, requiring customized preparation beyond generic system design knowledge.
- **Practice with real examples** using the 64-question practice bank covering search/retrieval and content moderation scenarios to internalize how to think about AI system design during interviews.
