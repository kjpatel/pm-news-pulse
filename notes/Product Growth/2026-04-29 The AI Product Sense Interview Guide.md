# The AI Product Sense Interview Guide

**Source**: [Product Growth](https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/ai-product-sense-guide)
**Author**: Aakash Gupta | **Date**: Apr 29, 2026

---

## Summary

AI product sense interviews have evolved significantly since October 2025 and are now a critical evaluation round across top tech companies, with questions requiring deep knowledge of AI capabilities and agentic workflows rather than traditional product frameworks. This guide provides updated interview strategies, real mock examples, and specific tactics for excelling in these high-stakes rounds that determine offer level and compensation.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prepare AI-specific frameworks**: Traditional product sense frameworks like CIRCLES no longer work for AI product sense questions—you must understand agentic workflows, model capabilities (like Opus 4.6), and AI-native product surfaces instead of pattern-matching your way through answers.
- **Expect AI evaluation everywhere**: AI product sense is spreading beyond dedicated rounds; even if your recruiter doesn't mention it, expect AI fluency to be evaluated within traditional product sense interviews, so prepare differently than you would for standard PM interviews.
- **Prioritize the AI round for leverage**: The AI product sense round (not behavioral) is the true determinant of offer level, compensation, and negotiation leverage—this is where L4 vs L5 placement decisions are made, making it the highest-stakes interview.
- **Master current AI products deeply**: Successful candidates demonstrate deep knowledge of specific AI products and their technical constraints (Claude Code's capabilities, Cowork infrastructure, model limitations) rather than generic product strategy.
- **Practice with real curveballs**: Top companies throw mid-interview pivots to test your flexibility and real-time problem-solving; practice mocks that include unexpected constraints or direction changes to prepare for this evaluation style.
