# How PMs Can Take Advantage of Karpathy's Second Brain

**Author**: Aakash Gupta | **Date**: Apr 13, 2026

## Summary

Andrej Karpathy's viral post on building a personal knowledge system addresses PMs' struggle to retain and leverage research across projects, offering a practical framework to preserve institutional knowledge and avoid repeated discovery cycles.

## Key Takeaways

- **Systematize research storage** by implementing a personal knowledge management system that captures user interviews, competitive analysis, and stakeholder context in a centralized, searchable location rather than relying on ephemeral notes.
- **Create a reusable research repository** that allows you to reference past findings across quarters and projects, reducing duplicate work and enabling faster decision-making on similar problems.
- **Build institutional memory** by documenting discovery insights in a structured format that survives project transitions and helps future iterations learn from previous research investments.
