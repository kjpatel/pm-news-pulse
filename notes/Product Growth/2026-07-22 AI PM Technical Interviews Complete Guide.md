## Summary

This guide provides AI product managers with comprehensive preparation for technical interview rounds at major tech companies, covering real questions asked at OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Nvidia, and other firms, along with core concepts and winning answer strategies.

## Key Takeaways

- **Study core AI concepts** like transformers, hallucinations, RAG systems, and GPU infrastructure—these are the technical foundations consistently tested across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google DeepMind, and Nvidia interviews.
- **Prepare system design answers** that demonstrate ability to architect end-to-end AI experiences with clear trade-off analysis around latency, relevance, cost, and model selection.
- **Build practical agentic systems** before interviews—companies like Glean and Google explicitly ask candidates about end-to-end agentic systems they've built to solve real problems.
- **Master the evaluation framework mindset** including human-in-the-loop feedback loops and prompt chain prototyping, as companies like Meta specifically assess this capability.
- **Practice explaining technical depth** by going several layers deep into ML systems and AI infrastructure, demonstrating diagnostic and problem-solving ability rather than surface-level knowledge.
