## Summary

PMs need to build two intelligence layers—personal and team-based—to stay informed and make better decisions, and Anthropic's new automation tools enable efficient setup of these systems without heavy engineering overhead.

## Key Takeaways

- **Build a personal intelligence layer** that aggregates competitor moves, user feedback, and stakeholder context so you're never the last person to know critical information
- **Implement team-level systems** that surface relevant data to users and stakeholders automatically, reducing manual communication bottlenecks
- **Use Anthropic's automation tools** to set up these intelligence layers quickly without requiring significant engineering resources or complex workflows
